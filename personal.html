Beyond teaching and academics, I have quite a few hobbies that generally fall under the umbrellas of athletics and nerdom.

As an athlete:

Ultimate

I play Ultimate competively, including professionally, high-level club, and internationally. My recent teams include Madison NOISE (2025), Sublime at TEP (2025; sponsored by Sublime Ultimate), Family Style (2024), and the Los Angeles Aviators from 2019-2021. But my first true love in Ultimate is playing on the beach! I also coach with Ultimate Peace. And look to further expand Ultimate into Indigenous communities and international communities.

Juggling

I have also been a semi-active juggler since 2009. My best friend in high school was one of the world's best jugglers at the time and we spent many hours every week working on the ultimate craft for introverts. The picture to the right (PC: Casey Rentmeester) is a classic 5 club cascade (cascade = the normal juggling pattern for an odd number of objects).

My personal records are 9 balls, 7 rings, and 6 clubs. Presently, when I juggle I like to work on passing patterns (2 or more people juggling together by throwing objects back and forth) with 6, 7, and 8 clubs, or to play combat with 3 clubs.
