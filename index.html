Welcome to Kyle Conniff's website!

I am a Ph.D. candidate of statistics at the University of California Irvine (UCI). I am working under the direction of Dr. Dan Gillen in censored suvival data for Native American health care. I received a Bachelor's of Arts from St. Norbert College (SNC) in mathematics in 2016. I strive to develop materials and resources for teaching statistics and mathematics to marginalized students and communities.
